The Glass-Box Study Pipeline.

ZenNode is an enterprise-grade AI study environment designed by and for neurodivergent learners. Convert sprawling lectures, fragmented notes, and chaotic thoughts into highly structured, un-overwhelming mastery sheets.

Architectural Principles

Zero Friction Ingestion

Native macOS Menubar app. Click to record global system audio or transcribe thoughts on the fly. No CLI wrestling required.

The Glass-Box Method

AI should synthesize, not hallucinate. Every concept is broken into first-principles definitions, mental models, and "junior pitfalls" designed specifically for ADHD cognitive patterns.

Obsidian Integration

Writes directly to your Obsidian vault. Leverages the Zettelkasten method by atomically appending new mastery nodes to your personal knowledge base.

Enterprise Durability

Built on LangGraph with SqliteSaver checkpointing. Features LLM fallback routing, structured Pydantic validation, and comprehensive automated prompt testing (Promptfoo).
